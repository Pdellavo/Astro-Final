Astronomy Equations

Parallax Equation

Distance (parsecs) = 1 / Parallax angle (arcseconds)

Flux Equation

Flux = Luminosity / (4 * π * Distance²)

Luminosity Equation

Luminosity = Flux * (4 * π * Distance²)

Wien's Law

Peak Wavelength (nm) = 2.898 * 10⁶ / Temperature (K)

Doppler Equation

Doppler Shift (nm) = Wavelength * (Speed / Speed of Light)

Newton's Law of Gravitation

Gravitational Force (N) = (G * Mass₁ * Mass₂) / Radius²

Escape Velocity

Escape Velocity (m/s) = √((2 * G * Mass) / Radius)
